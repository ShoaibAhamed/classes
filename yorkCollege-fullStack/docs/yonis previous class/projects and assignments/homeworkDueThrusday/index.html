Nissan GTR

The Nissan GT-R is a 2-door 2+2 high performance vehicle produced by Nissan unveiled in 2007. It is the successor to the Nissan Skyline GT-R although no longer part of the Skyline range itself, the name having been given over to the R35 Series and having since left its racing roots.

Infiniti Q60

The Infiniti Q60 is a sport luxury coupe and convertible manufactured by Infiniti, as the successor to the Infiniti G coupe and convertible.

Ferrari 488

TThe Ferrari 488 (Tipo F142M) is a mid-engined sports car produced by the Italian sports car manufacturer Ferrari, introduced in 2015 to replace the previous 458. The car is essentially an update to the 458 with major exterior and performance changes.

SLS AMG GT Final Edition

Available in both Coupé and Roadster body styles, the SLS AMG GT final edition is the final limited production iteration (350 units total, 25 in Japan) of the SLS AMG GT. Features includes an exposed carbon fibre hood with central air outlet, carbon fibre front splitter on the front apron, fixed carbon fiber rear spoiler from the SLS AMG Coupé Black Series, the choice of 2 exclusive AMG forged light-alloy wheels.

BMW M6

The first generation M6 was critically acclaimed throughout its lifespan for its elegant, aggressive "shark-nose" styling, its luxury equipment and its performance. A top speed of 158 mph (254 km/h)[3] made the European M635CSi the second fastest BMW ever built next to the M1.
